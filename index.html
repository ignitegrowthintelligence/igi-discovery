Enter a prospect's domain. Discovery gathers the evidence — their ads, their search demand, their reputation, their foot traffic — and builds a hypothesis-driven brief around the 7 discovery elements.

Building the brief…

Data handling: Briefs are built from public sources (Google Business Profile, Meta Ad Library, Google Ads Transparency Center, the prospect's website, licensed keyword data, Placer.ai foot-traffic estimates) plus Townsquare's IGI Library. Evidence sections render source data directly with as-of dates; strategy sections are AI interpretation and are labeled as such. No client audio or personal data is collected by this tool. Cached intelligence is stored in company-managed infrastructure.
